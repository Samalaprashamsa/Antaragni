Jitterburg

Jitterbug, the flagship dance event of Antaragni. This is a Western Group Dance Competition. Participants take part
in team of 5-35 and perform different types of Western Group dance in this dance competition.

As a part of Antaragni, Dance Events are conducted every year with full fervour and witness intense participation
from dance enthusiasts all over the nation.

Dance Events: Jitterbug, Estampie, Nrityangana, Dance Battle
